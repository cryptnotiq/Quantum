About

Quantum Capital was founded by Entrepreneur and Investor Ian Worrall to manage the financial and personal affairs of the Worrall Family. Ian acquired his wealth largely through early-stage investments in cryptocurrencies, blockchain technologies, and decentralised protocols related to DeFi, Proof of Stake, and Derivatives.

Investments

Quantum Capital continues to invest heavily in the web3.0 ecosystem and focuses on projects designing new, innovative economic models which bridge entrepreneurship, finance, and social impact. In addition, Quantum Capital invests in a mix of traditional and alternative assets throughout Europe, Asia, and Oceania.

Contact Us

Quantum Capital is headquartered in Prague with a presence in Singapore.
